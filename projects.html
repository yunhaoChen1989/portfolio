Poker Game

This is our final project for JAVA, We build a poker game using javafx
The rules of Poker Game are very simple
it is a one player game where the user will be dealt three cards, and the CPU will be dealt three cards as well.
You cannot see each others' cards you can swap your cards to match up against a different card of the CPUs.
Then once you press battle your cards will be to battle together and the person with more larger cards wins.
You are offered to play again once the game is finished.
